Visiting Student @ Chosun University

July 2019 - August 2019

I was fortunate enough to receive an award of Global Korea Scholarship (GKS) which I was able to conduct short research program at Chosun University, South Korea. I was assigned to CAD/CAM/CAE Lab where I had a wonderful time to work with undergraduates from other countries. I also met my supervisor as he was doing his PhD threre.<

Group Photo Session with Prof S.S. Kim (me on most left)

Our team was assigned to print several dumbbell-shaped specimen with different widths and special designs at the testing section to test the tensile strength of printed specimen.


The specimen was modelled using CATIA V5 and printed using Makerbot printers. After that, UTM (could not remember the model) was used to test the specimens. For further information, please have a look at poster.
