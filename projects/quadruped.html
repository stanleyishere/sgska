Quadruped Robot Design
November 2020 - August 2021

This project is a mandatory final year subject to partially fulfil the degree requirement. My project supervisor was Ir. Dr. Chua Bih Lii. Since I was actively invovled in making robots, I specifically chose quadruped robot as my project title.

The quadruped robot is a five-bar linkage leg with direct drive mechanism.
